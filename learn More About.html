Learn More About SFARET Technologies

Overview

SFARET Technologies is an innovative startup specializing in advanced educational assistance applications aimed at helping students, administrators, and academics in the education sector. We leverage cutting-edge AI technology and automation chatbots to provide solutions that cater to the administrative needs of educational institutions, teachers, and students.

Our services include applications that manage student projects and studies, organize educational events, provide virtual support to educators, develop educational materials, and streamline administrative processes. We are committed to revolutionizing educational administrative support with AI-driven solutions that simplify tasks, save time, and increase productivity for educational professionals and students.

Headquartered in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria, SFARET Technologies is structured as a Company and founded by a team of experts, including Dike Paul (Founder) and the SFAME RESEARCH TEAM, with backgrounds in education, academic consulting, and technology.

Vision Statement

To enable the educational ecosystem and professionals to thrive by simplifying tasks, saving time, and fostering productivity through advanced AI technology.

Mission Statement

Empowering the educational ecosystem through advanced technology to create flexible, user-friendly learning and administrative experiences that meet the evolving needs of educational institutions.
